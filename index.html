About us

A serve oriented non-profit foundation, lending a hand to humanity by extension of the United Nation's Sustainable cities and communities (SDGs 1, 4 and 11 respectively).

We are desirous to making impacts in young peoples lives by reducing poverty levels in the society through the provision of quality education. We also advocate for a safe and clean environment for all, reduction in juvenile crime rate and elimination of drug abuse through our humanitarian gestures.
